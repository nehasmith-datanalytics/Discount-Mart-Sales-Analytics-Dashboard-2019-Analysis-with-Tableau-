# Discount-Mart-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-2019-Analysis-with-Tableau-

A comprehensive Tableau dashboard analyzing DiscountMart’s 2019 sales performance across time, product categories, purchase quantities, and geographic regions. This project showcases data storytelling through visual insights and KPI tracking.

##  Objective:

To uncover sales trends, category performance, customer buying behavior, and regional strengths using interactive visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs). The goal is to support strategic decision-making and identify growth opportunities.

##  Key KPIs:


- What was the **total sales revenue** generated in 2019?  
- How much **profit** did DiscountMart earn? 
- How many **sales transactions** were completed?
- What was the **average monthly sales figure**?
- Which **product category** contributed the most revenue?
- In which **month** did sales peak?
- Which **state/region** recorded the highest sales? 

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

- **Monthly Sales Trend:** Line chart showing fluctuations and Q4 surge.
- **Category Breakdown:** Horizontal bar chart comparing Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture.
- **Purchase Quantity Distribution:** Histogram revealing dominance of small-basket purchases (2–5 units).
- **Geographic Sales Map:** State-wise sales heatmap highlighting regional performance.

## Tools Used:

- **Tableau Public** – for dashboard creation and interactive visualizations  
- **Excel / CSV** – for initial data preparation  
- **Data Cleaning & Transformation** – performed within Tableau  
- **GitHub** – for version control and project documentation

##  Insights:

- Q4 sales surge driven by November and December.
- Technology leads category sales, but Office Supplies and Furniture show strong support.
- Majority of purchases are low-quantity, indicating frequent small orders.
- California, Pennsylvania, and New York are key revenue-generating states.
